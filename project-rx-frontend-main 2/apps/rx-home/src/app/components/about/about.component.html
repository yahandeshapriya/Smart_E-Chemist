Welcome to Smart E-Chemist Pharmacy! We are a locally-owned and operated pharmacy that has been serving our community for over 20 years. Our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable medications to our patients, along with friendly and knowledgeable service. Our team is led by our pharmacist, John Smith, RPh, who has over 15 years of experience in the field. He is supported by a team of dedicated and experienced staff members, who are committed to helping our patients with all of their medication needs. We offer a wide range of services to our patients, including prescription filling, over-the-counter medication sales, and drug delivery. We also have a strict process of how we acquire our drugs from our supplier and make sure that it is safe and FDA approved.
